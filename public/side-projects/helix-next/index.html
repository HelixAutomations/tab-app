What the app is about

The app is not just a Teams tab.

It is Helix Law's internal operational workspace. It tracks prospects, turns pitches into instructions, manages compliance and matter opening, and increasingly acts as the control plane for what the client later sees in the portal.

Design direction

Helix should feel premium and operational.

Dense where speed matters, calm where judgement matters, and always clearer than email threads, spreadsheets, and ad-hoc status chasing.

Product rule

Every screen should answer three questions.

  • What changed?
  • Who owns this now?
  • What is the next decisive action?

Why this lab exists
